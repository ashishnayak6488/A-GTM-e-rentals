About Us

We love bicycles. We love travelling. And, we want you to love them too! What started as a bicycle rental platform is now a complete ecosystem of bicycles. From two wheeler rentals to riding gears, from exclusive bicycle merchandise to road trips and tours, from bicycle refurbishing and maintenance to fleet management system - we have them all.

Our team of ninjas aim to bridge the gap between bicycle fanatics and their need for a perfect go-to for all their bicycling needs. Whether you’re a student in a new city or someone who just joined work - you can rent a bicycle for a day, a week or even a couple of months, leave your bicycle at the station for some good ol’ maintenance and pick up some exclusive merch too! Whether you’re a startup with a small sales team or a food delivery giant, a logistics company or a renowned restaurant - for everything that depends on two wheels - ‘A-GTM e-RENTAL BICYCLES hai na’!

We also host a frequent travel series called Wander A-GTM where we take you on an epic bicycle journey at amazingly affordable prices. Check our Facebook page for upcoming getaways and more!

Our mission is to make your spontaneous trips easy and affordable

We want you to ditch the tour guides and unkept promises to travel whenever you feel like you need a break. At A-GTM bicycles, you will rediscover the joy of travelling as we bring you the best handpicked and carefully curated tours, activities and attractions in and around your city. We give you the freedom to be spontaneous, because the best holidays are!
